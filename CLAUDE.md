- **Single SKU, single campus, single semester.** (Complexity kills founders.)
- **No hand fulfillment.** Snag handles last-mile delivery; cron/automation only.
- **Production Aug 1–7.** Any slip kills the launch timeline.
- **Founders on-site for production.** Quality control + photographic proof.
- **No discounting below $4.99.** Brand floor is real; margin exists at that price.
- **Founder bandwidth:** both are full-time students; this is Phase 1 proof, not a business yet.
